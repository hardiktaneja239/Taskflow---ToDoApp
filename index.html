How to Use TaskFlow

Click the "Add New Task" button at the top of the page. Fill in the task title (required), add an optional description, choose a priority level, and click "Add Task". Your task will appear in the list immediately.

Click the checkbox icon on the left side of any task. The task will be marked as completed with a strikethrough style. Click it again to mark it as active.

Yes! Click the edit icon (pencil) on any task to open the edit modal. You can update the title, description, and priority level, then save your changes.

Click the trash icon on the right side of any task. A confirmation will appear to prevent accidental deletions. Confirm to permanently remove the task.

Yes! All your tasks are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. They'll be there when you return, even after closing the browser. Note: Clearing browser data will remove your tasks.
